About me

Currently working as an independent journalist in Addis Ababa, I focus on covering social, political, and international affairs with a commitment to in-depth storytelling.

Previously, I worked as a journalist in Saudi Arabia, both independently and as a correspondent for DW Radio. Over the years, I have reported on key global and regional issues, bringing critical stories to light through investigative journalism.

My expertise includes political analysis, human rights reporting, and uncovering stories that often go unheard. Passionate about impactful journalism, I strive to provide accurate and insightful coverage.

For work inquiries, contact: nsirak@gmail.com
